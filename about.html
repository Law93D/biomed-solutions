About Us

Who We Are!

Biomed Solutions, is a dynamic medical technology service provider committed to strengthening healthcare systems through reliable, affordable, and sustinable biomedical support. Established in Lesotho, our operations span equipment repair, preventive maintenance, technical support and the supply of medical equipment and consumables.

Our goal is simple: keep life saving equipment working optimally - because healthcare doesn't wait. We are driven by a vision to empower healthcare systems with innovative technology. We pride ourselves on offering a diverse and essential product portfolio that reflects the real needs of healthcare facilities across Lesotho. Our product range includes diagnostic, therapeutic, surgical, and patient care equipment, as well as the consumables and accessories needed for daily operations.

Our Expertise!

Biomed Solutions offers a comprehensive range of biomedical services and supplies tailored to meet the unique healthcare needs of Lesotho. We bring specialized expertise in sourcing, installing, and maintaining a wide range of medical, laboratory, and dental equipment.

Our team has hands-on experience with complex medical gas infrastructure—such as PSA oxygen plants, manifolds, and MGPS networks—as well. We offer in-depth consultancy, training, and compliance support to ensure every solution is functional, safe, and aligned with healthcare standards. With a strong focus on operational reliability and client satisfaction, we deliver practical, tailored solutions that keep critical systems running efficiently.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in transforming healthcare delivery across Southern Africa — through innovative technologies, sustainable practices, skilled local talent, and strategic partnerships that bridge gaps in medical access, safety, and service excellence.

Our Mission

To support and strengthen healthcare systems by delivering high-quality medical and laboratory equipment, sustainable consumables, expert consultancy, and hands-on technical services. We are committed to empowering local professionals through training and partnering with public and private stakeholders to build resilient, efficient, and equitable healthcare infrastructure.
